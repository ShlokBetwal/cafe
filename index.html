Our Story

Born from a passion for minimalist design and deep, authentic flavors, our cafe brings together premium stone-ground Japanese matcha infusions with artisan comforting dishes. We provide an aesthetic sanctuary where every detail—from our warm earthy textures to our handcrafted menus—is designed to help you slow down, relax, and savor your morning brew or evening gathering.

📍 Visit Us: Near Iscon Cross Road, Ahmedabad, Gujarat
